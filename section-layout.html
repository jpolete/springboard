Layout

Styles for layout containers.

Section 7.1
Constrained

Constrains a content block to a maximum width and pads it with white space.

This block won't expand any wider than 65em, and will remain centered in its container.

HTML

<div class="constrained">
  <p>This block won't expand any wider than 65em, and will remain centered in its container.</p>
</div>
